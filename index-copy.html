Life At The Depot!

Thank you for visiting life at the depot.com. Conveniently located The Depot offers the unique opportunity to be within walking distance to businesses, residential living, grocery shopping, restaurants, and West Texas A&M University (GO BUFFS!!!).

The Depot has been designed from the beginning to bring people, business and life together in a way never before seen in Canyon by combining residential living with office/retail all in one great location. Residents also benefit from nearby WT, United Supermarket, Feldman's Wrongway Diner and more. Future residents please call or stop in to view our one bedroom, two bedroom, or loft apartment offerings.

Ammenities include:

  • 2 bedroom/2 bath
  • Granite counter tops
  • Stainless appliances
  • Gas stove
  • Personal garage
  • 24 hour parking surveillance
  • washer dryer hookups



Please contact us today to reserve your spot in what is sure to become Canyon's most talked about new development.


Loft Apartments

Residents enjoy 24/7/365 parking surveillance, granite counter tops, stainless appliances, gas stove, personal one car garage, along with great views overlooking beautifully-landscaped courtyards and all the excitement at The Depot.
